STUDENT PORTAL APPLICATION


The Student Portal Application System provides administrators, teachers, and students with a number of services that are meant to improve access to educational resources, communication, and organization. Typical services include the following:

Course Registration: Online course scheduling, course availability, and registration are all available to students. Grades & Academic Records: Via the site, students can safely view their grades, transcripts, and academic records. Assignment Submission:

Through the system, teachers can give feedback and grades to students as well as receive assignments and projects electronically. Communication: The system offers message features, discussion forums, and announcements as ways for administrators, instructors, and students to get in touch with one another. Resource Sharing: Various educational resources, including reading materials, multimedia content, lecture notes, and presentations, may be accessible through the portal.
